Flockin' Demons

A game by Bewelge

Made in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 55

Instructions

You play as a necromancer.

Use your mouse to move. Use Pentagrams to summon minions that will fight enemies for you.

Minions will flock around you. If you hold down your left mouse button they will swarm the nearest enemy.

If a minions defeats an enemy it will heal and level up, becoming stronger.

Survive as long as you can!

Oh dear, the bad demons have taken over!
